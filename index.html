Watching

At PyDemy, we believe that the first step to learning is getting familiar with the concept. Thus, we post videos on our YouTube channel that help you understand the concept. Most of these videos are in the format of an instructor explaning the concept and then solving problems using the same concept.

  • Our YouTube Channel

Reading

Now that the learner is familiar with the concept, they should be able to read about the concept with ease. Usually, most of the concept is discussed in the video itself, however some extra details might be included in the article. Articles include a video of the concept at the top, images to help the learner understand, and a link to a practice exam at the end of the article.

Practicing

Once the learner reaches the end of the article, a button connecting the article to the practice exam would show up. Practice exams are created to allow the learner to measure their understanding of the subject. Most practice exams consist of 5-8 questions. Your score will be revealed after you submit an exam, along with a video on how to solve each question.

Our Courses

PyDemy offers a variety of courses related to programming and mathematics that you can choose from.

Algebra 1

Develop a proficiency in the fundamentals of Algebra that are vital to your future mathematical pathways.

Geometry

Explore various concepts ranging from logic & reasoning to 2-Dimensional and 3-Dimensional figures.

Algebra 2

Enhance your prior knowledge of Algebra by exploring the "second chapter" of Algebra, unlocking many new functions and concepts.

Precalculus

Apply your knowledge of Algebra and Geometry to construct a foundation for future Calculus courses.

Web Development

Dive in to the internet where you will learn how to create and use static & dynamic websites, webservers, and databases.

Python & Circuitry
